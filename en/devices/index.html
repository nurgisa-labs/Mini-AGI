./first pilot
Pilot in development

Detect presence without an always-on camera.

Multisensor presence detector

The device combines six independent signals to distinguish an empty room, movement and a stationary person. The result includes a confidence level and the reason behind the decision.

Privacy boundary: the microphone does not record speech — the device measures only the overall noise level. A camera is not part of the base pilot.
